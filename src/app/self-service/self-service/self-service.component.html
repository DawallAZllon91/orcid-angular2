We're updating ORCID Self Service

As of June 1, 2022 , Self Service will no longer be available through the ORCID Registry.

An enhanced version of Self Service will be added to the ORCID Member portal in the comming year. All the features currently available will be transferred to the portal, along with additional new functionality

info
While we build this improved version for the Member Portal, Self Service will be unavailable.

What to do next

Should you need any of the Self Service features during this time, please reach out to membership@orcid.org for assistance.
